Did or does Savannah go to university?

You betcha! Savannah goes to the University of Waterloo and studies Geomatics within the Faculty of Environment (currently in her fourth and final(!!!) year). But, you might be wondering...what is Geomatics? According to Wikipedia, Geomatics is "the discipline of gathering, storing, processing, and delivering geographic information, or spatially referenced information". This still might not make sense but, Geomatics is actually a widespread discipline that encompasses things like satellite imagery, geographic information systems (GIS), and web mapping.
